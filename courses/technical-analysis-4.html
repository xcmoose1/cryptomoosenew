1. Common Trading Biases

Confirmation Bias

The tendency to search for information that confirms your existing beliefs.

  • Only looking for bullish signals in an uptrend
  • Ignoring contradictory information
  • Following echo chambers

FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)

Making impulsive decisions based on the fear of missing potential gains.

  • Buying at market tops
  • Increasing position size irrationally
  • Ignoring risk management

2. Emotional Control

Protection Strategies

  • Stick to your trading plan
  • Use stop losses religiously
  • Keep a trading journal
  • Take regular breaks
