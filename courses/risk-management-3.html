Stop Loss Strategies

Learn effective stop loss placement and management

Course Progress: 50%

1. Types of Stop Losses

Fixed Stop Loss

  • Set at specific price level
  • Based on technical levels
  • Clear risk definition
  • Easy to calculate position size

Trailing Stop Loss

  • Moves with price action
  • Locks in profits
  • Based on indicators/ATR
  • Dynamic risk management

2. Stop Loss Placement

Optimal Placement

  • Below/Above key support/resistance
  • Outside price swings
  • Beyond market noise
  • Consider volatility (ATR)
