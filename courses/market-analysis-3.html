2. Volume Analysis Techniques

Bullish Volume Signals

  • Rising price with rising volume
  • Volume climax after downtrend
  • Positive volume divergence
  • High volume breakouts

Bearish Volume Signals

  • Falling price with rising volume
  • Volume climax after uptrend
  • Negative volume divergence
  • Low volume rallies
