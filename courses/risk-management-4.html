Positive Correlation

  • Similar price movements
  • Increased portfolio risk
  • Example: BTC & Large caps
  • Risk multiplication

Negative Correlation

  • Opposite price movements
  • Risk reduction
  • Example: BTC & Stablecoins
  • Portfolio hedging
