1. Understanding Market Types

Bull Market

  • Upward price trend
  • High trading volume
  • Strong market sentiment
  • Increasing market caps

Bear Market

  • Downward price trend
  • Lower trading volume
  • Negative sentiment
  • Decreasing market caps

2. Market Analysis Methods

Technical Analysis

  • Price action
  • Chart patterns
  • Technical indicators
  • Volume analysis

Fundamental Analysis

  • Project evaluation
  • Team assessment
  • Tokenomics
  • Market potential
