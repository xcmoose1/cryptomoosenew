1. Introduction to Chart Patterns

Chart patterns are graphical representations of trader psychology and market movements. Understanding these patterns is crucial for predicting potential price movements.

2. Support and Resistance

Support and resistance levels are key concepts in technical analysis that help identify potential price reversal points.

Support Levels

Price levels where downward trends tend to pause due to concentrated demand

  • Historical price bottoms
  • Psychological round numbers
  • Moving averages

Resistance Levels

Price levels where upward trends tend to pause due to concentrated supply

  • Previous price peaks
  • Fibonacci retracement levels
  • Trend lines
