2. Fibonacci Retracements

Fibonacci retracement levels are powerful tools for identifying potential support and resistance levels.

Key Fibonacci Levels

  • 23.6% - Minor retracement
  • 38.2% - First major level
  • 50.0% - Mid-point (not Fibonacci)
  • 61.8% - Golden ratio
  • 78.6% - Deep retracement

Application Tips

  • Draw from swing low to high
  • Combine with trend lines
  • Look for confluence
  • Use in multiple timeframes
