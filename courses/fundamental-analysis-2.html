Project Team Analysis

Learn how to evaluate the team behind a crypto project

Course Progress: 33%

1. Team Background Check

Key Team Members

  • Founders & Co-founders
  • Technical Team
  • Advisors
  • Partners

Verification Sources

  • LinkedIn Profiles
  • GitHub Repositories
  • Previous Projects
  • Social Media Presence

2. Red Flags

Common Warning Signs

  • Anonymous team members
  • Fake LinkedIn profiles
  • Limited technical experience
  • History of failed projects
  • Inconsistent information

3. Team Assessment Exercise

Case Study: Project X

CEO Background

  • 10 years in blockchain
  • Previously at Major Tech Co.
  • Active GitHub profile
  • Speaks at conferences

CTO Background

  • Anonymous identity
  • Claims 15 years experience
  • No verifiable history
  • Limited social presence

Based on this information, what is your assessment of the team?

A) High risk - CTO anonymity is a major red flag
B) Low risk - CEO's background compensates
C) Medium risk - More investigation needed
D) No risk - Common in crypto
