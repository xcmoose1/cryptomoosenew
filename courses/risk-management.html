2. Risk-Reward Calculation

Risk-Reward Formula

Risk-Reward Ratio = (Take Profit - Entry) / (Entry - Stop Loss)

Example:
Entry: $100
Stop Loss: $90
Take Profit: $130

Risk-Reward = (130 - 100) / (100 - 90)
           = 30 / 10
           = 3:1 ratio
