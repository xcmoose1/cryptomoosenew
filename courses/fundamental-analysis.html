Learn how to evaluate crypto projects based on fundamentals

Course Progress: 16%

1. What is Fundamental Analysis?

Definition

Fundamental analysis is the evaluation of a cryptocurrency's intrinsic value by examining related economic, financial, and other qualitative and quantitative factors.

Key Components

  • Project Team Analysis
  • Tokenomics
  • Market Analysis
  • Technology Assessment
  • Community & Adoption

2. Why is it Important?

Benefits

  • Long-term investment decisions
  • Risk assessment
  • Project viability evaluation
  • Market opportunity identification

vs Technical Analysis

While technical analysis focuses on price patterns and indicators, fundamental analysis looks at the underlying factors that drive value.
